Who uses the admin sidebar day-to-day?

Understanding the roles will shape which items need to be front and center vs. tucked away.

A

Mostly you (owner/manager)

Supervisory view — you're watching what's happening, not starting production yourself. Floor staff use tablets at their stations.

B

You + a small office team

2–3 people use this admin. Some do receiving, some check reports, some manage products. Different roles, same sidebar.

C

Mixed — admin and floor leads

Floor leads also use this side (not just the tablet floor UI) for things like starting production runs or pulling up workflow status.
